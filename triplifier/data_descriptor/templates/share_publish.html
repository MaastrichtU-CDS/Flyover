Benefits of hosting mappings in Git
Opting to host your mappings on a Git-tracked system is a great way to keep track of changes in your mapping over time, and to easily share updates with your collaborators.
You can even host the various mappings of your project in different branches and gain insight into what concepts are available amongst project partners without any data access concerns.

Health DCAT-AP Metadata Export

Generate Health DCAT-AP metadata describing your project's variables, values, and relationships.
This format ensures EHDS compliance and enables standardised health data sharing.

What is a Health DCAT-AP file?
A Health DCAT-AP file is a standardised metadata format based on the Data Catalogue Vocabulary (DCAT) Application Profile for health data.
It provides a structured way to describe datasets, their variables, and the relationships between them, facilitating data sharing and interoperability in the health domain.
