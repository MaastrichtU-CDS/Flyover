Welcome to Flyover

A privacy-aware tool to transform your data into F.A.I.R. semantically interoperable resources

Transform your data into F.A.I.R. semantically interoperable Resource Description Framework (RDF) triples through our guided four-step process

1. Ingest

Upload and process your datasets to prepare them for semantic annotation and interoperability.

  • CSV and DB support
  • Automatic conversion
  • Data grooming
  • Link multiple graphs

2. Describe

Provide metadata and descriptions for your data variables to enhance semantic meaning.

  • Data type specifications
  • Unit specifications
  • Local definitions
  • Variable labelling

3. Annotate

Annotate your data using standardised ontologies to make your data semantically interoperable.

  • Ontological annotations
  • Semantic interoperability
  • Metadata-driven
  • Value mapping

4. Share

Share (meta-)data of your semantically interoperable data and speed up your collaborations.

  • Archive mapping
  • Generate mock data
  • Increase findability
  • DCAT-AP export
