Welcome to Flyover 🐦

Your Data FAIR-ification Tool for Clinical Datasets

Transform your clinical data into semantically interoperable RDF format through our guided three-step process

1. Digest

Upload and process your clinical datasets to prepare them for semantic annotation.

  • CSV and PostgreSQL support
  • Data validation and preprocessing
  • Automatic RDF conversion
  • Primary/Foreign key relationships

2. Describe

Provide metadata and descriptions for your data variables to enhance semantic meaning.

  • Variable categorization
  • Unit specifications
  • Local definitions
  • Data type mapping

3. Annotate

Apply semantic annotations using standardized ontologies to make your data FAIR.

  • Ontology-based annotations
  • Value mapping to terminologies
  • Semantic interoperability
  • FAIR data principles

About Flyover

Flyover is a comprehensive Data FAIR-ification tool designed specifically for clinical datasets. It converts structured tables into Resource Description Framework (RDF) format, ensuring your data follows FAIR principles (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable).

The tool supports both CSV files and PostgreSQL databases, providing flexibility for various data sources and formats.
